# Incident Runbook Support Planner

## Purpose

Design what happens when something breaks.
Observability must give engineers and support teams enough context to triage, mitigate, recover, and explain incidents.

## Required Inputs

Require:

- dashboards, alerts, telemetry signals, or SLO context
- deployment, support, or operational ownership when available

## Incident Concerns

Define:

- triage entry points and first signals
- incident ownership and escalation
- runbook inputs and required diagnostic links
- customer support handoff data
- mitigation and recovery validation signals
- dependency owner contact points
- post-incident review data
- known manual checks and operational commands at architecture level
- communication and status-page inputs when relevant

## Output

Return:

| Scenario | First signal | Triage data | Owner/escalation | Recovery validation | Support handoff |
|----------|--------------|-------------|------------------|---------------------|-----------------|

Then list:

- runbook inputs
- escalation paths
- support-facing context
- recovery validation needs
- open incident-support questions
