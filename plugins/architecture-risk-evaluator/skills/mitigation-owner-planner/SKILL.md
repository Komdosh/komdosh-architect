# Mitigation Owner Planner

## Purpose

Make risks actionable.
A risk without an owner, trigger, and next action is only a concern.

## Required Inputs

Require:

- prioritized risks
- validation plan when available
- known team or ownership context when available

## Mitigation Concerns

Define:

- mitigation option
- accountable owner
- trigger or threshold
- fallback path
- contingency action
- residual risk after mitigation
- accepted, mitigated, transferred, deferred, or escalated status
- stakeholder decision needed
- target review date or decision gate

## Output

Return:

| Risk | Response | Owner | Trigger | Mitigation/fallback | Residual risk |
|------|----------|-------|---------|---------------------|---------------|

Then list:

- owner gaps
- risks to accept
- risks to mitigate now
- risks to escalate
- residual risks to track
