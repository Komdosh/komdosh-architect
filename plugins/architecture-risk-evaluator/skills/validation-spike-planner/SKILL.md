# Validation Spike Planner

## Purpose

Turn uncertainty into evidence.
Some risks should be validated before the team commits to implementation.

## Required Inputs

Require:

- prioritized risks
- assumptions or unknowns to validate

## Validation Types

Consider:

- technical spike
- proof of concept
- benchmark or load test
- integration probe
- data sample analysis
- security review or abuse test
- operational runbook drill
- stakeholder decision workshop
- vendor/provider capability check
- migration or rollback rehearsal

## Output

Return:

| Risk | Validation work | Success signal | Timebox | Owner | Decision gate |
|------|-----------------|----------------|---------|-------|---------------|

Then list:

- validations needed before commitment
- validations that can happen during implementation
- risks that cannot be validated cheaply
- evidence needed for go/no-go
- open validation questions
